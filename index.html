Welcome

Welcome to our Bird Watching site.
We're so glad you're here!

Birds Watching can also be defined as what we called "Birds Eye Perspective"
where we creating a view as if the observer were a bird in flight looking forwards.

People cannot fly in their own compared to the literal birds,
but we can see ahead of our present - it is to sight the future by making a bird's view.

By choosing our website, you're now part of a small community of individual
who read, listen, share, communicate with others all over our country via Birds Watching.
Whether you've joined to create, share, listen or engage to the others stories,
we've got something for you!
